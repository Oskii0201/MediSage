Project Description and Requirement:
Your project is an opportunity to apply Deep Learning and AI concepts to real-world problems. This hands-on experience will demonstrate your understanding of the course material while building practical skills.
 
You are asked the build an AI system consists of at least:
1) A Deep Learning model
2) LLM based Agent
3) User Interface (it can be visual frontend or just CLI)
You need to make a presentation and submit a report using the attached template:
Your presentation should contain technical  details and real-time demonstration of your system
 
Grading will be as follows:
15%- Problem definition and dataset analysis
20% - Novelty of the approach and importance of the problem
15% - System/solution design and explanations of the reasons behind design selections
15% - Explanation of the techniques used and developed
15% - Preliminary analysis of the performance evaluation for the techniques used
20% - Structure, clarity, and completeness of the presentation
